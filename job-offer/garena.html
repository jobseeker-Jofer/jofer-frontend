Job Description

Network Engineers develop network architectures that support the high bandwidth and low latency requirements of our products and games. They ensure that the data center network is always efficient, robust, scalable, and secure.

  • Analyze requirement and perform network solutioning, planning, installation and setup of network systems
  • Provide network operation support, problem determination, and troubleshooting for network incidents and escalate to vendor and management accordingly
  • Review and verify the design and implementation of the network solution
  • Manage the inventory of network devices
  • Maintain network performance by performing network monitoring, analysis, and performance tuning
  • Create and maintain documentation related to network configuration, policies, procedures and service records
  • Participate and provide technical support in network implementation projects wherever and whenever required

    Minimum Qualification

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Science/Information Technology or relevant field
  • Good understanding of routing protocols like OSPF and BGP
  • Basic Systems administration experience with Linux
  • Experience with Nexus, Cisco, and Juniper switches is a plus
  • CCNA, CCNP, CCIE is a plus
  • Good communication and coordination skills with both internal and external stakeholders
  • May be required to provide on-call support
